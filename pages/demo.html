Under the Hood

How the Demo Works

What happens between your drawing and the prediction result.

1. Draw

You draw a digit on the 280×280 HTML5 canvas using mouse or touch.

2. Capture

The canvas is captured as a PNG image and encoded to base64.

3. Groq Vision

The image is sent to Groq Vision AI (Llama 4 Scout) which reads and identifies the digit.

4. Output

Groq returns the predicted digit with confidence scores for all 10 classes (0–9).
